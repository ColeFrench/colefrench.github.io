Robotics

I'm a member of a robotics team called 2 Train Robotics. Check out our website here.

2 Train Robotics is among thousands of teams participating in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition, a competition dedicated to educating youth in the fields of science, technology, math and engineering. Each year, teams of students design and manufacture a robot which must accomplish a goal based on a given theme in just six weeks. Different teams collaborate with each other while competing fiercely, creating an environment of support and rivalry known as gracious professionalism.
